SelfieBot

DIY Endurance Robot Educational Robotics Program. Programmable robot for education.

Robot for beginners, intermediate and high education.
Affordable – Less than US$100
Programmable – Arduino based.
Compatible with other – Can be used in a combination with other robots
Easy to operate – Remote control, Follow me (tracking function), demo mode.
DIY SelfieBot robot is a kit that is designed for students and colleges, for self taught and young hobbyists for improving skills in robotics. We provide a full introduction and an extended guidance how to make a robot with your own hands.

Practical and useful for classrooms, robotics teams, science projects, self learning (DIY), talented students, home studding.
